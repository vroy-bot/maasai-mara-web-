Departments

  1. School of Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences and Creative Industries
    Focuses on literature, history, philosophy, sociology, political science, communication, and creative arts. Enhances critical thinking and prepares students for media, public administration, and education careers.
  2. School of Natural Resources, Environmental Studies and Agriculture
    Covers sustainable resource management, conservation, environmental science, and agriculture. Offers research and training in agribusiness, forestry, and climate change adaptation.
  3. School of Business and Economics
    Specializes in business management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship. Focuses on economic principles, financial planning, and strategic business operations.
  4. School of Education
    Trains future educators in curriculum development, psychology, and special education. Produces graduates contributing to national and international education systems.
  5. School of Pure and Applied Sciences
    Includes mathematics, physics, chemistry, and biological sciences. Engages in scientific research and innovation, preparing students for careers in medicine, engineering, and technology.
  6. School of Tourism and Hospitality
    Provides training in tourism management, hotel administration, event planning, and customer service. Focuses on hospitality skills and sustainable tourism.
  7. School of Health Sciences
    Offers nursing, medical laboratory science, public health, and clinical medicine programs. Prepares students for healthcare and medical research professions.
