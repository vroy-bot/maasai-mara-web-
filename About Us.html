Welcome to Maasai Mara University

Maasai Mara University is a public university that is the only university in Narok County, situated 150km in the South West of Nairobi en route to the great Maasai Mara Game Reserve in Kenya. The university provides high-class quality education for all; focusing on innovative teaching based on research, with global collaborations with industries and other institutes. The University has specialized in the following disciplines: Business, computing, ICT, education, psychology, tourism, environmental sciences, and health sciences and research. So get to know about the university and its programs through our website and all our social media links.
