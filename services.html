Welcome to Maasai Mara University

Maasai Mara University is a public institution and the only university in Narok County, situated 150 km southwest of Nairobi, near the great Maasai Mara Game Reserve. We focus on innovative teaching and research, with global collaborations in various disciplines.

Our Academic Disciplines

We specialize in the following fields:

  • Business and Economics
  • ICT and Computing
  • Education and Psychology
  • Tourism and Hospitality
  • Environmental Sciences
  • Health Sciences

Our programs are designed to equip students with practical and theoretical knowledge to excel in their careers.

Our Vision

To provide quality university education through innovative teaching, research, and consultancy services for sustainable development.
